# Privacy Policy for GOHelper

**Last Updated:** April 30, 2026

GOHelper ("the App") is provided as a utility for players of mobile games. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**No Personal Data Collection:** The App does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal identifying information (PII). 

**On-Device Data Storage:** Any data you create within the App—including saved search presets, custom tags, and appraisal settings—is stored **locally on your device** using an internal database (Isar). This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App fetches publicly available game data (such as current Raid Boss rotations and game statistics) from external URLs (GitHub/JSON repositories). 
*   These requests do not include any personal information about you.
*   The App does not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or advertising networks.

## 3. GDPR and User Rights
Under the GDPR, users have the right to access, rectify, or erase their data. Since all data is stored locally on your device:
*   **Access/Rectification:** You can view and edit your saved presets and tags directly within the App.
*   **Erasure:** You can delete all App data by:
    1. Deleting individual items within the App.
    2. Clearing the App's storage in your Android System Settings.
    3. Uninstalling the App.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It is a utility tool designed for players of mobile games.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
